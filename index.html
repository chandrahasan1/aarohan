AAROHAN A Community Learning Center

Aarohan community learning centers act as vehicles of education, learning, innovation and creation for all the members of the community. It not only offers definite scientific, artistic, sporting arrears for the kids but also similar interest oriented, job oriented vocational help for adults. We will be looking at taking up community projects that engage the community and enhance their understanding of their own problems

In short, it is a place for the community members to join together to plan, develop and be part of activities that are local, friendly, welcoming, non-judgmental and, most importantly, engaging and involve learning for all the parties involved.

Aarohan has been founded and managed by alumni of Teach for India and BITS Pilani who are passionate about engaging low income communities in nation building. The team at Aarohan showcases varied strengths in diverse sectors of education, leadership, community engagement and entrepreneurship. These skills are supported by a strong alumni network of their reputed alma mater organizations. Aarohan currently operates in a low income community of Delhi providing after school education to government school kids and livelihood skills training to their mothers.

Aarohan: A Community Learning Center

Aarohan is a one stop-learning hub for all the community members. It addresses the issues around the communities in the following manner:

  • After school program for children which supplements school teaching to further deepen their concepts and enhance their skills coupled with an added advantage of developing life skill and social skills which will enable them to use their academic knowledge in a better manner.
  • The center provides relevant skill building courses such as sewing, stitching and embroidery for parents to help them earn sustainable livelihoods.
  • The center focuses on extracting, building and using local human capital of the community. It also creates an avenue for sharing among different community members

Organisation

Goal

To create self-sustainable community centers that provides meaningful education to children, imparts livelihood skills and generates employment for women making them independent. We aim to ensure a nation building process by providing quality education to all and mobilizing the potential work force in low-income communities.

Vision

To create holistic learning hub for all the members of the community and provide everyone a valuable learning opportunity in order to create dynamic, self-sustainable and knowledgeable societies

Mission

  • To provide quality education through after school learning centers that focus on holistic development of stakeholders seeking education
  • To educate students so that they are able to explore both the world and their inner being
  • Imparting necessary skills to youth and women in order to build entrepreneurial attitudes and help them establish their own micro-enterprises

Collaborations

Akanksha

The Akanksha Foundation is a non-profit organization with a mission to provide children from low-income communities with a high-quality education, enabling them to maximize their potential and transform their lives. We are gratified by the support provided by Akanksha by sharing their curriculum during our inception. We are working around it so as to tailor it according to the needs of our kids.

SEWA (Self Employed Women's Association)

SEWA Delhi is a non profit organization committed to organising women workers in the informal economy to protect their livelihoods and work towards their development through skill development and creating market linkages, developing microfinance institutions, linking with social security programmes and promoting skill and education among their daughters and children. SEWA has provided us with curriculum for the 6 month tailoring and embroidery program that we provide as a part of livelihood skill training for female parents of children.

AES (American Embassy School)

The American Embassy School serves students from the United States and other nations. It provides a quality American education that enables students to be inspired learners and responsible global citizens through the collaboration of a dedicated faculty and a supportive community. We are glad to have received considerable support in terms of resources and mentorship from AES. We look forward to positively impact the community we are serving through mentored guidance of AES.

Teach for India

Teach For India, a project of Teach To Lead, is a nationwide movement of outstanding college graduates and young professionals who will commit two-years to teach full-time in under resourced schools and who will become lifelong leaders working from within various sectors towards the pursuit of equity in education. Being a social start up by the alumni of Teach for India, Aarohan does get its share of support from the brand for what the alma mater organization stands for.
